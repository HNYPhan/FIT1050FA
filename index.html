About Us

We are a highly invested club that work for the same goal, that is to provide members and individuals with the best experience in this extreme sport. This is no normal club, but rather an open ground for those who like to demonstrates their strength and ability. Inspired by the Hunger Game Trivial, we would like to bring club members with the closest experience to the sport of man-hunting, but rather with a friendlier approach. So why the wait?
